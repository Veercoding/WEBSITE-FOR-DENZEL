Most trusted in our field

Star Credit Limited is a Microfinance Institution in Western Kenya Region with Head Office in Kisumu City on the shores of Lake Victoria bordering South Sudan, Uganda and Tanzania. It provides financial and other related support services to entrepreneurs (individuals, groups and companies) in the rural and peri-urban areas that are socio-economically marginalized. Star Credit Limited is predominantly rural focused and based institution where challenges such as poor or near no infrastructure systems, low literacy levels and general poor economic not only remain low but untapped as well. The institution focuses on the low income members and entrepreneurs in the rural and peri-urban areas especially Women and Youth in Western Kenya region with plans to serve entire country.
